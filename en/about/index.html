Emergency Care Deployment

Context

Massive increases in the demand for emergency care can overwhelm the healthcare system, requiring a rapid reorganization of services offered to the local population.

Solution

ClicHealth supports the deployment of non-traditional healthcare sites and available resource management in service centres.

ClicHealth makes it possible to group & manage service centres together to effectively distribute key services and information throughout a region.

Appointment allocation mechanisms make it possible to customize service access criteria in order to prioritize vulnerable individuals.

Control

Healthcare Centre Security

Context

One of the main challenges facing healthcare authorities is limiting and reducing the risk of infection at the point of service to protect both the public and healthcare professionals.

Solution

ClicHealth is equipped with an appointment planner module that reduces crowding and respects social distancing regulations.

The auto-triage function manages appointment distribution according to an individual’s current health conditions. ClicHealth ensures the safe management of symptomatic cases without compromising the safety of patients and medical staff.

Automatic Distribution

Automatic Distribution

Context

During a pandemic, “walk-in” clinics increase crowding, increase the risk of infection and increase wait times.

Solution

ClicHealth reduces strain on the healthcare system by adding access channels via the internet, smartphones and video-conferencing.

With ClicHealth, patients book appointments, confirm reservations and receive automatic reminders and follow-ups all online.

Time savings generated by the automatic distribution of health services allow critical physical and human resources to be reassigned to strategic locations to support surges in demand.

General Health

General Health Services Module :

A pandemic forces the reevaluation of the mechanisms for accessing healthcare. “Walk-in” or “drop-in” care no longer meets patient and staff safety requirements.

The General Health Services Module can meet the challenge:

  • Automate appointment distribution and follow-up
  • Control congestion and eliminate lines
  • Improve customer service and minimize travel
  • Avoid crowding and respect social distancing norms
  • Sort and manage symptomatic cases
  • Multiply access channels (tablet, mobile, video-conferencing)

  • Promote appointment self-management

ClicHealth automates and controls the distribution of appointments in specimen collection centres, routine nursing care, youth clinics, vaccination clinics, radiology services, pediatric clinics and assorted other health programs.
